Frequently Asked Questions

What is Peace Pigeons?

Peace Pigeons is a community-driven memecoin project with the mission to unite peace supporters worldwide. We believe in using blockchain technology to amplify peaceful initiatives and charities.

How can I get Peace Pigeons tokens?

You can participate in the prelaunch phase to get early access to Peace Pigeons tokens. More information will be shared soon.

What makes Peace Pigeons different?

Our project is focused not just on the memecoin culture, but on real-world impacts. A portion of all proceeds will go towards charities that promote peace and non-violence.

When will the token officially launch?

The official launch is planned after the prelaunch phase. Stay connected with us for updates!

How do I get involved?

Follow us on our social media platforms, engage in our community, and participate in the prelaunch to help us build momentum for Peace Pigeons.
